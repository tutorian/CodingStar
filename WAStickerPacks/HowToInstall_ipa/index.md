# How to install "ipa"-files?

**You need:**

*   computer / laptop
*   your iPhone
*   [Cydia Impactor](http://www.cydiaimpactor.com/)
*   Lightning Cable

--------------------------

1.  Downloading Cydia Impactor and the "ipa"-file.
2.  Open Cydia Impactor and connect your iPhone with your pc.
3.  Drag&Drop the "ipa"-file into the window.
4.  Enter your Apple ID and password.
5.  Wait until the process is completed.
6.  On your iPhone, go to Settings -> General -> Profiles & Device Management -> (your apple id) -> Trust developer -> Trust
7.  Done!
